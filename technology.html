Technology

Innovation meets perfection

Italian Engineering Excellence

For over three decades, Audison has been at the forefront of car audio innovation, combining traditional Italian craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Our commitment to excellence drives every aspect of our design and manufacturing process, from the initial concept to the final product that reaches your vehicle.

Our Technologies

Advanced DSP

32-bit floating point digital signal processing with advanced algorithms for perfect sound tuning and time alignment.

Thermal Management

Innovative cooling systems and thermal protection ensure optimal performance and longevity of our amplifiers.

Class D Efficiency

High-efficiency Class D amplification technology delivers maximum power with minimal heat generation.

Protection Systems

Comprehensive protection circuits safeguard against short circuits, overheating, and overloading.

OEM Integration

Seamless integration with factory audio systems while maintaining all original functionality.

App Control

Advanced mobile applications for real-time tuning and system monitoring from your smartphone.

Research & Development
