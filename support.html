Warranty Info

Frequently Asked Questions

You can register your product online through our warranty registration portal. You'll need your product serial number and purchase receipt. Registration must be completed within 30 days of purchase for full warranty coverage.

Audison products come with a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. This covers manufacturing defects and component failures under normal use conditions.

While some products can be installed by experienced DIY enthusiasts, we recommend professional installation for optimal performance and to maintain warranty coverage. Our dealer network includes certified installation specialists.

Firmware updates can be performed using the Audison DRC software or mobile app. Download the latest firmware from our support section and follow the step-by-step instructions in the user manual.

Vehicle-specific wiring diagrams are available in our technical database. You can access these through our dealer portal or contact our technical support team for assistance with your specific vehicle model.
