About Audison

The art of sound since 1979

Our Story

Founded in 1979 in Italy, Audison has been at the forefront of car audio innovation for over four decades.

What started as a passion project by a group of audio enthusiasts has evolved into one of the world's most respected car audio brands. Our commitment to excellence, Italian craftsmanship, and relentless pursuit of perfect sound has made us the choice of audiophiles and professionals worldwide.

From our headquarters in Modena, Italy, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in mobile audio, creating products that don't just play music â€“ they bring it to life.

Our Journey

1979
Foundation

Audison is founded in Italy by a group of passionate audio engineers with a vision to create the perfect car audio experience.

1985
First Amplifier

Launch of our first high-end car amplifier, establishing Audison as a premium brand in the mobile audio market.

1995
Digital Innovation

Introduction of our first digital signal processor, revolutionizing car audio tuning and system integration.

2005
Global Expansion

Expansion into international markets, bringing Italian audio excellence to customers worldwide.

2015
Thesis Series

Launch of the Thesis series, our most advanced and prestigious product line, setting new standards for performance.

2024
Future Forward

Continuing to innovate with new technologies and expanding our commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices.
